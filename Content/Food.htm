Food

Macon has quite a versatile plethora of eateries. There of course are the classic burger joints, like the Rookery or Ocmulgee Brew Pub, but there are also more unique places like Spud Dogs and Parish. Also be sure to check out the Macon staples like H&H and The Bears Den. These are just a few restaurants that Macon has to offer, so go out and explore! Even just by walking around downtown, you can find places for all types of tastes.

 

The Rookery

The Rookery is just about one of the best places to find an amazing burger and shake. Located on Cherry Street, right in the heart of downtown, it has a classic vibe that any food loving individual will enjoy. Each burger is named after either a classic musician or Macon staple. The Rookery also has a small intimate live music venue where you can see some of the best bands around middle Georgia while eating some of the best food around.

 

 

Ocmulgee Brewpub

Ocmulgee Brewpub is another classic place to find quality burgers. Each burger is handmade daily from fresh ingredients. The mix of industrial machinery and wooden picnic tables creates a wonderfully fresh atmosphere. Ocmulgee Brewpub is located on 2nd Street, just off of Cherry Street.

 

 

 

Spud Dogs

Spud Dogs is one of those places you won’t quite understand until you’ve been there. It’s got a hole in the wall vibe with the food to live up to it. As you can imagine, just about everything on the menu includes either potatoes or hot dogs. If you’re looking for a truly unique place to eat, check out Spud Dogs on Cherry Street.

 

 

 

Parish

Parish is where you go for cajun food and classy upstanding service. Just like the Rookery, Parish has live music and a relaxing atmosphere, but with a rustic-chic vibe. Many people say you either like the Rookery or Parish, so which is it for you?

 

 

 

 

H&H

Macon has a handful of well known staples that you must visit if you have the chance. H&H is probably the most significant. H&H serves traditional southern comfort food in the classic hole in the wall style for breakfast and lunch. H&H is woven into Macon’s history, most notably with the Allman Brothers Band, so if you want historically great soul food, check out H&H on Forsyth Street.

 

 

 

The Bears Den

The Bears Den, located on Oglethorpe Street not even a mile from Mercer University, is another of Macon’s traditional southern comfort food eatery. The Bears Den provides a mix of a cafeteria and home cooking atmosphere with its well known dishes like mac n’ cheese and fried chicken & collards.
