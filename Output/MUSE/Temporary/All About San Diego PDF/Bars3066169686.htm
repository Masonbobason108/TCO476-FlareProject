Bars

Macon’s bar scene is one of many options. It’s got unique bars such as Reboot Retrocade & Bar where you can drink classic beers while playing classic arcade games. Macon also has the traditional bars with a long list of beers on tap such as Just Tap’d. And just about every bar will have some sort of live music too! We’ve compiled a list of some of the best bars in town, but be sure to go out and explore what other bars Macon has to offer.

 

Reboot Retrocade & Bar

Reboot Retrocade & Bar calls themselves the “Nerdiest bar in town”, so if you’re into games of any kind, they’ve probably got them. Be sure to check them out for a truly unique bar experience.

 

 

 

 

 

Just Tap'd

With 64 beers on tap, you’re sure to find something to love at Just Tap’d. You can sit inside the rustic and earthy bar, or just outside at the street side seating and enjoy Macon’s downtown atmosphere.

 

 

 

 

 

Growler USA

With an assortment of beers, wines, ciders, and many others, Growler USA is one of the best places to go if you’re near Mercer. Growler USA is especially a great place to go if you’ve just been at Mercer for a football game. Their traditional bar feel will certainly leave you satisfied.

 

 

 

 

The Hummingbird

The Hummingbird, affectionately called “The Bird”, is one of the better known bars here in Macon. It’s probably equally known for its beer as it is for its live music. The Hummingbird is probably the most classic bar in town with trivia nights, darts, and steller live music. Be sure to stop by The Bird for a truly Macon experience.
